Krushi Kendra System - Software Requirements Specification (SRS)

1. Introduction
The KrushiKendra.com is a web-based application that aims to provide a digital platform for farmers and agricultural stakeholders to access information, services, and resources related to agriculture. The system will provide a knowledge hub, facilitating communication and collaboration among farmers, experts, and government organizations to enhance agricultural practices and productivity.

The Krushi Kendra System will provide the following functionalities:

Farmer registration and authentication.

Crop and livestock management guidance.
Weather information and forecasts.
Market prices and trade information.

3. Functional Requirements

1 Farmer Registration and Authentication
Farmers will be able to create an account by providing their personal details such as name, contact informations, and location.
The system will validate and authenticate farmers' accounts during registration and login.

2 Agricultural Information and Updates
The system will provide access to a wide range of agricultural information, including crop cultivation techniques, pest control measures, fertilizer usage, etc.
Farmers will be able to browse and search for relevant articles, and research papers related to agriculture.
The system will allow farmers to subscribe to receive regular updates and news.

3 Crop and Livestock Management
The system will provide guidance on crop selection, planting schedules, irrigation methods, and harvesting practices based on the farmer's location and other relevant factors.
Farmers will be able to record and monitor crop-related activities such as fertilization, pest control, and yield management.
The system will provide information and best practices for livestock management, including feeding, breeding, and healthcare.

4 Weather Information and Forecasts
The system will have weather APIs to provide real-time weather information, including temperature, rainfall, humidity, and wind conditions.
Farmers will be able to view weather forecasts for their specific location .

5 Market Prices and Trade Information
The system will provide farmers with access to market prices for various agricultural commodities.


6 Government Schemes and Subsidies
The system will provide information on government agricultural schemes, subsidies, loans, and support programs.
Farmers will be able to access eligibility criteria, application procedures, and documentation requirements for various schemes.

7 Communication and Collaboration Features
The system will communicate and collaborate among farmers, experts, and government officials through variousd ways, chat features, and whatsapp  groups.
Farmers will be able to ask advice, share experiences, and connect with agricultural experts. 

8 Online Training and Learning Resources
The system will provide access to online training modules, videos, and educational resources related to agriculture.
Farmers will be able to enhance their knowledge and skills from various  training programs.

 Non-Functional Requirements

1 Usability
The system will have an user-friendly interface.
It should be accessible and usable on both desktop and mobile devices.

2 Security and privacy
User data will be stored securely and protected .
The system will implement proper authentication and authorization method.

3 Performance
The system will be able to handle
a large number of concurrent users and provide responsive performance.
Data retrieval, search, and information updates should be fast and efficient.

4 Reliability
The system will be highly reliable and available for farmers to access information and services at all times.
It will have appropriate backup and disaster recovery mechanisms.

5 Scalability
The system architecture will support scalability to accommodate increasing user and data demands.
It should be able to handle future growth and additional features.
